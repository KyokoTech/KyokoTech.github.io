Create Your Own Coloring Books

Color by Kyoko is one of our flagship projects, developed entirely in-house by our expert team. It's a unique platform that allows users to create custom coloring books and share them with a vibrant community. From concept to execution, we built Color by Kyoko from the ground up, showcasing our ability to quickly deliver fully customized solutions that are both powerful and user-friendly.

Here's a peek under the hood of how it all comes together:

  • Hosted on AWS: We chose AWS for its scalability and reliability, ensuring the platform runs smoothly, no matter how many users are coloring or creating. AWS also allows us to easily scale as demand grows, keeping performance high and downtime low.
  • Stripe for Payments: To handle secure transactions, we integrated Stripe as our payment processor. Users can purchase tokens through the platform, allowing them to unlock special features and create their own custom stories, all while enjoying a seamless and secure payment experience.
  • AI-Powered Customization: Leveraging our expertise in AI, Color by Kyoko includes cutting-edge AI tools that help users generate unique designs and patterns, making every coloring book a personalized masterpiece.
