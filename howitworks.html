Our Process

At Kyoko Technologies, we believe in a collaborative approach that ensures your vision is brought to life efficiently and effectively.

Here's how we turn your ideas into a working reality:

  1. Free Consultation: Understanding Your Needs

    We start with a conversation.

    • Initial Meeting: Our team will meet with you to understand your vision, goals, and requirements. This consultation is free of charge.
    • Needs Assessment: We'll dive deep into what you want to achieve, discussing specific features, user experience, and potential challenges.
    • Solution Outline: Together, we will outline the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) that will serve as the foundation for your project.
  2. Development Phase: Building Your MVP

    Watch your idea come to life.

    • MVP Agreement: Once we finalize the MVP details, our skilled developers will begin crafting your application.
    • Bi-Weekly Updates: Throughout the development process, you'll receive bi-weekly updates on our progress, including demonstrations of new features and the opportunity for feedback.
    • Quality Assurance: Our team conducts thorough testing to ensure your MVP is not just functional, but also robust and user-friendly.
  3. Iterative Improvement: Enhancing Your Solution

    We don't stop at the launch.

    • Live MVP: Once your MVP is live, we continue to work with you to iterate and improve based on user feedback and performance metrics.
    • Ongoing Support: Throughout the development process, you'll receive bi-weekly updates on our progress, including demonstrations of new features and the opportunity for feedback.
    • Design and Brand Enhancement: We are partnered with a professional design team allowing us to elevate the visual appeal of your application. We can refine user interfaces, enhance branding, and implement modern design trends to ensure your product not only functions well but also captivates users with its aesthetic appeal.

Pricing Structure

We believe in transparency when it comes to pricing:

  • Free Consultation: Start here, no cost to you.
  • Upfront Payment: Our pricing starts with an upfront payment that covers initial implementation and infrastructure costs. The total amount will depend on the complexity of the solution being developed.
  • Monthly Charges: After the MVP is live, we will forward monthly infrastructure costs and charges to you, allowing for ongoing support and scalability.
  • Profit Sharing Option: For clients with limited funding options, we offer the opportunity to work out a profit-sharing agreement. This arrangement will last for as long as we continue to support the project, aligning our success with yours.
  • Handoff Costs: When you decide to take full ownership of your project, we will facilitate a smooth transition. There is a cost associated with the handoff, which covers the time and resources needed to train your new team and ensure they are well-equipped to manage the project independently.
